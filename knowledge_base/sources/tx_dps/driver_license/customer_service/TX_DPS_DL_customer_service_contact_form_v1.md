## What this page is
This is the Texas DPS Driver License Customer Service “Contact Us” web form used to submit inquiries about driver license and identification services.

Important operational note:
- The form requires reCAPTCHA completion prior to submission.

---

## Contact Form — What the user selects / submits

### Inquiry categories (multi-select)
The page instructs the user to “Please select the option(s) that most closely relate to your inquiry,” including:
- Appointment
- CDL
- DL/ID Inquiry
- Driver License Eligibility
- Driver Record
- Other

### Questions / description box
- Users must enter their question(s) and provide as much information as possible.
- The field is required.
- The page indicates allowed characters (alphanumeric and a limited set of punctuation).

## Additional Contact Information (phone support)
If unable to email or if the customer wants to speak with a specialist directly, DPS provides:

Customer Service Center phone:
- 512-424-2600

Hours:
- Monday – Friday, 7:00 am to 5:30 pm (excluding holidays)

Notes:
- Calls are routed via an automated menu to direct callers to the right team.
- Potential wait times can exceed 20 minutes during peak times due to high call volume.
- TIP for faster service: call during non-peak time, 7:00 am to 8:00 am.
