# Texas DPS — FAQ Section 2: Scheduling A Road Test

Source: https://www.dps.texas.gov/section/driver-license/faq/section-2-scheduling-road-test  
Publisher: Texas Department of Public Safety (DPS)  
Page date: 2020-11-18  
Retrieved at: 2026-01-22  

---

## Q1. Can the Customer Service Center schedule my road test for me?
No. The Customer Service Center cannot schedule road tests. You can schedule, reschedule, or cancel your driving appointment online:
- Schedule an Appointment: https://www.dps.texas.gov/section/driver-license/driver-license-services-appointments
- Scheduler: https://www.txdpsscheduler.com/

---

## Q2. Do I have to apply for a driver license before I can schedule a driving exam?
Yes. You must apply for a driver license before scheduling a driving exam. DPS recommends reviewing/printing the checklist:
- What to bring when applying for a Texas Driver License or Identification Card (DL-15 PDF): https://www.dps.texas.gov/internetforms/Forms/DL-15.pdf

DPS also recommends reviewing the application steps here:
- Apply for a Texas Driver License: https://www.dps.texas.gov/section/driver-license/apply-texas-driver-license

---

## Q3. How far in advance can you schedule a road test?
A road test can be scheduled up to 180 days in advance using the online appointment solution:
- Scheduler: https://www.txdpsscheduler.com/

---

## Q4. Do I have to provide my own vehicle for the driving test?
Yes. You must provide a vehicle and proof of insurance for that vehicle for the road exam.

---

## Q5. Do I have to be listed on the insurance policy for the vehicle I am using in my road exam to take the exam using that vehicle?
You do not have to be listed on the policy, but you cannot be listed as an excluded driver.

---

## Q6. I recently purchased a vehicle and it still has the paper license plates. Can this vehicle be used for my driving exam?
Yes, as long as the plates indicate they are buyer plates (not dealer plates).

---

## Q7. I'm from another state and my vehicle does not have a front plate, can I still use this for my driving test?
Yes. You may use a vehicle registered in another state, provided the vehicle complies with that state’s registration laws.

---

## Q8. Can I schedule a driving test for my child who is over 18 years old?
Yes. If you have the correct login information, you can schedule the exam through the online appointment solution:
- Scheduler: https://www.txdpsscheduler.com/

---

## Q9. What vehicles do I need to bring to my motorcycle exam?
You must provide:
- the motorcycle, and
- a separate vehicle and driver to follow you during the driving test for a motorcycle license.

---

## Q10. I still cannot find the answer to my question, what do I do?
If you do not find the answer within the FAQs, you can contact the DPS Customer Service Center:
- Contact page: https://www.dps.texas.gov/apps/DriverLicense/customer_service/Other.aspx
