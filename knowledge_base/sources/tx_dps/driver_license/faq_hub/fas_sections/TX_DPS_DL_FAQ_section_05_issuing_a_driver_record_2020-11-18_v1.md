# Texas DPS — FAQ Section 5: Issuing A Driver Record

Source: https://www.dps.texas.gov/section/driver-license/faq/section-5-issuing-driver-record  
Publisher: Texas Department of Public Safety (DPS)  
Page date: 2020-11-18  
Retrieved at: 2026-01-22  

---

## Q1. How do I order a driver record?
Driver records can only be ordered online or by mail. DPS directs customers to:
- How to Order a Driver Record: https://www.dps.texas.gov/section/driver-license/how-order-driver-record

You may also request your driver record by mail by completing and mailing the “Application for Copy of Driver Record (DR-1)” with the appropriate fee. If requested by mail, allow up to three weeks for delivery.
- DR-1 (PDF): https://www.dps.texas.gov/internetforms/Forms/DR-1.pdf

---

## Q2. I was charged twice for my driver record, how do I request a refund?
For payment issues, DPS instructs customers to email the E-Commerce Section, provide a brief explanation of the error along with contact information, and notes they will respond within 2 to 3 business days.

---

## Q3. I do not have my audit number and I need to submit my driver record to the court as soon as possible. What can I do?
If you do not have your audit number, DPS states you must request your driver record by mail by completing and mailing the DR-1 form with the appropriate fee:
- DR-1 (PDF): https://www.dps.texas.gov/internetforms/Forms/DR-1.pdf

DPS also advises contacting the court directly to determine whether they will grant additional time (an extension) to obtain the record.

---

## Q4. There is a crash on my driver record that is not supposed to be there. How can I get it removed?
DPS instructs customers to contact the Texas Department of Transportation (TxDOT) at (800) 558-9368.

---

## Q5. Which type of driver record do I need to order for defensive driving?
DPS indicates most courts require a Type 3A driver record, but you must contact the court directly to confirm which type is required for your case.
DPS links customers to additional information:
- How to Order a Driver Record: https://www.dps.texas.gov/section/driver-license/how-order-driver-record

---

## Q6. What is the password to print my driver record online?
The password to print your driver record is your driver license number.

---

## Q7. I still cannot find the answer to my question, what do I do?
If you do not find the answer within the FAQs, DPS directs customers to contact the Customer Service Center:
- Contact page: https://www.dps.texas.gov/apps/DriverLicense/customer_service/Other.aspx
