## Q1. I have a question about vehicle registrations and/or license plates.
For vehicle registration, vehicle titles, handicap placards, or vehicle plates, DPS directs customers to the Texas Department of Motor Vehicles (TxDMV) or to call TxDMV:
- TxDMV: https://www.txdmv.gov/
- Phone: 1-888-368-4689 :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}
