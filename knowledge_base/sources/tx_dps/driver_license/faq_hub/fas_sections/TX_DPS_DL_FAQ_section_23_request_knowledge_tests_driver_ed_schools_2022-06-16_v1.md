# Texas DPS — Section 23: Request Knowledge Tests by Driver Education Schools

Source: https://www.dps.texas.gov/section/driver-license/section-23-request-knowledge-tests-driver-education-schools  
Publisher: Texas Department of Public Safety (DPS)  
Page date: 2022-06-16  
Retrieved at: 2026-01-22  

---

## Q1. I am a certified Texas Department of Licensing and Regulation (TDLR) driver education school. How do I order the driver license knowledge tests to continue to test my students?
Email your request to: TPSTProgram@dps.texas.gov and include:
- School’s name
- School’s TDLR number
- Examiner’s name
- TDLR number of the requestor
- Email address of the school
- Requesting English only or both English and Spanish

Note: The test will no longer be provided by DPS General Stores on paper.

---

## Q2. What should I expect to receive?
Once your information is verified, you will receive an email response from DPS that includes the Class C knowledge exams in PDF format for your use.

---

## Q3. Can I share these with other schools?
No. These tests are for the sole use of your driver education school and must be maintained in a secure location. Any distribution outside of your organization is subject to action by DPS.

---

## Q4. Can I continue to order knowledge tests from General Stores?
No. DPS General Stores no longer provides driver license knowledge tests.

---

## Q5. Who should I contact if I have additional questions?
Contact Program Review at 512-424-5623 or email: TPSTProgram@dps.texas.gov
