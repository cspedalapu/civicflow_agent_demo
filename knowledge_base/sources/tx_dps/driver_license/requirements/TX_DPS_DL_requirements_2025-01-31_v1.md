## Key Notices
- DPS strongly encourages eligible customers to use Online Services for driver license transactions:
  - Online Services: https://www.dps.texas.gov/section/driver-license/online-services

- REAL ID guidance and document preparation:
  - REAL ID Checklist / Document Check tool: https://www.dps.texas.gov/apps/DriverLicense/RealID/

- In-office services:
  - All in-office driver license (DL) and identification (ID) card services are offered by appointment only.
  - Schedule an Appointment: https://public.txdpsscheduler.com/
